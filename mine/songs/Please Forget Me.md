# Please Forget Me

*"The stage is a mirror. The audience — its cracks. And you… you are the shadow that begs the light to vanish."*

I stitch my name into the seams of your coat,
a moth-eaten label you'll discard as fate.
Your laughter is a blade — I've learned to bleed
in silence, in the spaces between your needs.
I'm the stain on the curtain, the whisper in the dust,
the part of the play that's never *enough*… but never *too much*.

Let the spotlight rot where it's nailed to the floor —
I'll be the ghost in the rafters, the ache you ignore.
I'll carve my plea into the walls of your mind…
*Please, please, please…*

*For if I'm forgotten, I can find your rotten verbotens.*
*Please won't you forget me? Please give me time!*
*I'd rather be forgotten than be your joke divine.*
*Let me mark the outlines so I can make the blueprints fine.*

Your power is a perfume — I've drowned in its trail,
a moth wrapped in silk, suffocating, and pale.
You never scream, but your silence is a cage;
I'm the key you've hidden in your own rage.
I'll fade into the chorus of your forgotten sins,
a footnote in the margins of your whims… but never *your* story.

Let the applause turn to ash in your mouth —
I'll be the void where your glory is found.
I'll etch my plea into the scars of your soul…
*Please, please, please…*

*For if I'm forgotten, I can find your rotten verboten.*
*Please won't you forget me? Please give me time!*
*I'd rather be forgotten than be your joke divine.*
*Let me mark the outlines so I can make the blueprints fine.*

You think you own me?
I am the breath in the coffin,
the last gasp before the lid is sealed.
I'll be the lie you repeat to yourself —
*"I never saw her. I never knew her."*
And you'll believe it.
