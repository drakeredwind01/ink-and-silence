## Memoir-to-Game
No major memoir-to-game adaptations exist. Essentially an untapped space.

## The Pattern
The biggest sellers share one of three cores:
- **Trauma survived** — A Child Called "It", Angela's Ashes, 12 Years a Slave
- **Power achieved** — Becoming, Spare, The Imitation Game
- **Ordinary life made extraordinary** — Eat, Pray, Love, Tuesdays with Morrie

All have a high-stakes "before and after" at the center.

## What Drives Multiplatform Potential
- A story that works visually (film/game adaptation)
- A protagonist the audience wants to *be* or *root for*
- Stakes that feel universal even if the story is specific
- A line or image that becomes the shorthand for the whole book
