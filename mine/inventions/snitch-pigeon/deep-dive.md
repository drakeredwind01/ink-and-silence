# Snitch Pigeon — Deep Dive

## The Problem

Dating apps are designed to be used by single people. They are also used by people who are not single. There is currently no lightweight, relationship-native tool that gives couples soft visibility into each other's digital behavior without requiring full account sharing, location tracking, or the kind of invasive monitoring that signals a broken relationship rather than protecting a healthy one.

The gap isn't surveillance — it's the moment before you needed surveillance. The heads-up. The nudge. The bird.

---

## The Product

Snitch Pigeon is an AI desktop pet and cross-device loyalty companion. It lives in the corner of your screen — small, unobtrusive, animated — and monitors for behavioral signals that suggest one partner may be straying. When it finds something, it doesn't send a push notification. It flies to the other device, lands on screen, and delivers the information in character.

The delivery is personal, low-key, and always deniable. The pigeon is not a surveillance tool. He's a friend who happened to notice something.

---

## Core Behavior

### Detection
- Monitors for new dating app account creation (Tinder, Hinge, Bumble, etc.)
- Tracks unusual app installs or browser activity patterns
- Watches for behavioral shifts (sudden location privacy changes, app hiding, etc.)
- Operates passively in the background — not a tracker, a watcher

### Delivery
- When a trigger event fires, the pigeon animates across the screen and lands on the partner's device
- Delivers the tip in a whisper — puffed feathers, beak shielded, low voice
- Always maintains plausible deniability ("I'm just a bird, I don't know anything")
- Tone and language entirely determined by chosen skin

### Idle State
- When nothing is happening, the pigeon sits on a small animated stool
- Occasionally preens, blinks, shifts weight
- He is a stool pigeon. This is intentional.

## Skins

Each skin is a full personality replacement — same information, completely different delivery.

**Street Pigeon**
Black feathers, speaks in AAVE/ebonics. Invested. Dramatic. More upset about it than you are.
> "Aye. Aye. Look at me. Your girl — yeah. She out here on Tinder. I'm not sayin' nothin', but I'm sayin' somethin'. You feel me?"

**Ivy Pigeon**
Sweater tied around neck, speaks like a Princeton alumni. Clinical. Formal. Still a snitch.
> "I'm compelled to bring to your attention certain behavioral anomalies that suggest a potential breach of your mutual interpersonal covenant. This conversation, of course, never occurred."

**Sifu Pigeon**
Mandarin collar, speaks like Bruce Lee. Cryptic. Wise. Devastating.
> "The man who chases two women catches none. Your wife chases one app. Investigate. But you did not hear this from the bird."

**Holler Pigeon**
Tiny beard, open plaid shirt, Hurk from Far Cry energy. Doesn't understand technology. Found out anyway.
> "Now I don't rightly know what that there app is, but it ain't Sunday church, I'll tell you that much. You might wanna look into it, son. But Lord knows I didn't say a word."

*Additional skins planned. The framework supports any voice, accent, or archetype.*

## Platform

- **Mobile first** — iOS and Android, runs as an overlay app
- **Desktop companion** — Windows and Mac, lives in the corner of the screen
- **Cross-device sync** — partners link accounts; the pigeon lives on both devices simultaneously
- **Background process** — minimal battery/CPU footprint; activates on trigger events only

---

## Monetization

- **Free tier:** one default pigeon skin, basic detection (dating apps only)
- **Premium tier:** all skins, expanded detection (browser activity, app installs), snooze controls, delivery timing settings
- **Skin packs:** individual skin purchases for users who want specific personalities without full premium
- **Couples plan:** discounted paired subscription — both partners on one plan

## The Name

He's a snitch. He sits on a stool. He is, quite literally, a stool pigeon.

The name does double duty: desktop pet + informant archetype. The stool in his idle animation is not decoration. It is the joke, the brand, and the thesis statement of the entire product.

---

## What Makes It Different

Every other loyalty/accountability app frames itself as a tracker. A monitor. A leash.

Snitch Pigeon frames itself as a character. A friend. A little guy who happens to notice things.

The humor disarms. The personality creates attachment. The utility builds trust. And once he's saved you once, you'll never uninstall him — because now you know he's watching, and you know he'll tell you.

He just won't admit it.
