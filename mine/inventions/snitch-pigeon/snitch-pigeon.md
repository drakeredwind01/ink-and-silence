# Snitch Pigeon (The Stool Pigeon)

An AI desktop pet app for couples — a little pigeon that lives in the corner of your screen and watches out for suspicious activity.

## Concept

The pigeon lives on both partners' devices simultaneously. When it detects something suspicious (a new dating app account, Tinder activity, etc.), it flies over to the other partner's device and lands in the bottom corner of the screen to deliver the tip — quietly, with deniability.

## Personality

- Whispers like a nervous informant, puffing up its feathers to shield its beak
- Never admits it told you anything
- Sample line: *"Hey, psst. Over here. Your wife is doing something. Now you didn't hear it from me — but she might be doing some 'things' on Tinder. You might want to check things out, ok? But it wasn't me, right?"*

## Idle State

When there's nothing to report, the pigeon sits on a cute little stool and rests.

He's a stool pigeon. A stooly. That's the whole bit.

## Skins

Different pigeon personalities you can unlock or choose from:

- **Street Pigeon** — black feathers, speaks in AAVE/ebonics. Real one.
- **Ivy Pigeon** — sweater tied around its neck, speaks like a Harvard or Princeton alumni. Very concerned about your "interpersonal contractual obligations."
- **Sifu Pigeon** — mandarin collar, speaks like Bruce Lee. Philosophical. Cryptic. Still tells you your wife is on Tinder.
- **Holler Pigeon** — tiny beard, open plaid shirt, talks like a hillbilly. Hurk from Far Cry energy. Will absolutely mind your business.
- *...more skins to come*

## Key Features

- Monitors dating app activity across devices
- Cross-device communication (flies from one partner's phone to the other's)
- Proactive — tries to alert before something fully happens
- Animated desktop pet with character and humor
- Deniable delivery ("I'm just a bird, I don't know anything")
